MY MARVEL JOURNEY

I’ve always loved superhero movies, but I didn’t start watching Marvel films until 2020. I knew that if I committed, I’d want to experience the franchise from the very beginning. Thanks to Covid-19, I finally had the time to binge them all in release order. The first Marvel movie I watched was Iron Man, and to this day he remains one of my favorite heroes.
