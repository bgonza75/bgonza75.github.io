PHASE 4

  • Black Widow: 7.5/10; I think this film was pretty good. It definitely came too late but I enjoyed it. I wish we would have gotten more background on the Red Room.
  • Shang-Chi: 8/10; This movie was great. I loved all the fight scenes and the legend behind the ten rings.
  • Eternals : 7/10; I actually enjoyed The Eternals. Great characters, fascinating history but there are lots of questions left unanswered.
  • Spider-Man: No Way Home : 10/10;I can't even describe how AMAZING this movie is. I never thought I would see all 3 spiderman on screen together but it happened. Also, I am so happy we saw DAREDEVIL!
  • Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness: 6.5/10; I liked the introduction of America Chavez but wasn't a fan of Wanda's story, I feel like it contradicted the ending of WandaVision where she chose to save the town and not her kids.
  • Thor: Love and Thunder: 4/10; I'm not going to lie, didn't like this one. They tried recreating the humor of Ragnorak and failed. It's the truth.
  • Black Panther: Wakanda Forever: 9.5/10; Beautiful movie and portrayal of grief. I also really liked Namor's character and can't wait to see him in future projects.

PHASE 5

  • Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania: 3.5/10; So much potential wasted. I'm sorry it was just bad.
  • Guardians of the Galaxy Volume 3: 10/10; Loved this movie all around. Although it was heart-wrenching learning about Rocket's past it also was beautiful now that he has the Guardians. Best trilogy in the MCU!
  • The Marvels: 4/10; I liked seeing characters from the shows - and the post credit scene.
  • Deadpool & Wolverine: 8/10; What a GREAT movie - it was fun, sad, nostalgic. It was everything I expected. Loved seeing all the past heroes get the recognition they deserve!
  • Captain America: Brave New World: 8/10;Underrated movie. I really liked seeing Sam fully embrace the role of Captain America and am so excited to see more of him as well as the new Falcon.
  • Thunderbolts: 9/10; Beautiful story-telling and portrayal of depression. I think everyone can relate to Bob and feels for him. I loved this group's dynamic and the post credit scene has me super excited for the future of Marvel!

PHASE 6

  • Fantastic Four: First Steps: 9.5/10; Exceeded expectations. Loved this movie and how people got it wrong - thinking they weren't going to be able to save their world. There was just one part I wasn't a fan of, how they portrayed Franklin saving his mom was kinda goofy. Despite that one little thing I loved this movie and am excited to see these characters interact with my other favorites. In all transparency I was freaking out when I saw Doctor Doom at the end!!!
